Characters of Noli Me Tangere
Maria Clara Maria Clara de los Santos, also known by her full name, was Crisostomo Ibarra's fianc√©e and the daughter of Capitan Tiago. After Ibarra went back to San Diego, Maria Clara encountered a lot of opposition to their engagement.
Padre Damaso Was the real father of Maria Clara and the previous town clergyman of San Diego. The man attempted to keep his daughter and Crisostomo Ibarra apart for Maria Clara's benefit by opposing their marriage.
Kapitan Tiago Was the father of Maria Clara and an important businessman in San Diego. Capitan Tiago strained to follow the friars' instructions and betrothed his daughter to Crisostomo Ibarra.
Dona Victorina Was a socialite and Capitan Tiago's acquaintance. She and her husband Don Tiburcio aimed to network with San Diego's powerful people in order to elevate their social status.
Sisa Was the mother of Basilio and Crispin and a resident of San Diego. Sisa went wild after both of her sons vanished and wandered the town looking for them.
